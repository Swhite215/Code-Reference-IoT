Guide and Notes for Working with MQTT.fx

1. Getting Started
    - Open MQTT.fx
    - Configure Broker Settings
    - Connect to Broker (Local, Cloud, etc)
    - Subscribe to Topics
    - Optional: Publish to a Topic through UI
    - Monitor Subscriptions
2. Best Practices/Tips and Tricks
    - Use payload decoders for easy viewing
    - Use topics collector to see all topics on broker
        - Double click a topic to subscribe
